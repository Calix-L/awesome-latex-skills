# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive
- Give credit for others' work
- Focus on what benefits the community
- Assume good faith

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the project maintainer at zhenxinlin290@gmail.com. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
